Sciamma's modern drama feels simultaneously tender, tough, and true to life. Presented as part of The Tournées Festival.

    THE LOOK OF SILENCE

    2014. Denmark et al. Dir: Joshua Opp enheimer. With Documentary. 103 min.

    The standalone companion piece to 2012's shocking The Act of Killing pr opels audiences into the tragic tale of an eye doctor searching for the men who killed his brother during the Indonesian genocid es.
